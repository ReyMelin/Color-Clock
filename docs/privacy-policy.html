Privacy Policy

Color Clock – Wear OS Watch Face

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

Color Clock is an analog watch face for Wear OS built using the Watch Face Format (WFF). It runs entirely on your device and is designed with your privacy in mind.

Data Collection

Color Clock does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. The app:

  • Does not require an internet connection.
  • Does not collect analytics or crash reports.
  • Does not access contacts, location, health data, or any other sensitive information.
  • Does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies.
  • Does not contain advertisements.

Permissions

Color Clock does not request any runtime permissions beyond what is required by the Wear OS system to display a watch face.

Third-Party Services

Color Clock does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Color Clock does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
